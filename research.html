Research & Publications

Michelle studies how complexity shapes financial choices and how decision-support tools can improve outcomes in consumer and household finance.

  • “Improving life insurance decision-making: The role of calculators”, with Carsten Murawski and in collaboration with AIA Australia Ltd.
  • “What drives aversion to complex problem-solving”, with Carsten Murawski, Nitin Yadav, and Michele Garagnani. OSF pre-registration

Publication
