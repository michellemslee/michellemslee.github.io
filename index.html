Michelle Lee is a PhD candidate in Decision, Risk, and Financial Sciences at the Centre for Brain, Mind and Markets, University of Melbourne. Her research focuses on how complexity affects financial decision-making and how tools can be designed to help navigate such challenges, with a particular interest in decision sciences and consumer finance.

Michelle holds a Bachelor of Commerce in Finance and Marketing and an Honours degree in Finance from the University of Melbourne. She has experience in portfolio risk management, academic research, and university teaching across finance, analytics, and fintech.
